💌 A Letter From My Heart 💌

To The One Who Walked Into My Quiet World,
And Made It Louder — In The Best Way Possible,

You Remember That Silent, Introverted Girl I Used To Be?
The One Who Avoided Eye Contact, Kept Words Locked Inside, And Never Knew How To Take Up Space?
Well… She’s Still Here — But She Talks Now. A Lot.
Even To Strangers. And That’s Because Of You.

You Didn’t Just Become My Friend.
You Became My Loudest Cheerleader, Softest Listener, And The Warmest Corner Of My Chaos.
You Believed In Me Before I Knew How To Believe In Myself.
You Gave Me The Kind Of Confidence That Doesn’t Come From Compliments —
But From Being Seen, Accepted, And Backed Every Step Of The Way.

I Call You My Favorite Sirdard — Because Honestly,
Only You Have The Guts To Interrupt Me Mid-Sentence Just To Watch Me Glare.
And Then? One Look. One Innocent Face. And Everything’s Fixed.
Fooshhh — All My Anger Disappears.

You Fight For Me.
You Care For Me — Even When I Push You Away.
Even When I Throw Tantrums, Act Dramatic, Or Pretend I Don’t Care…
You Stay. With Patience, With Kindness, With Love That Doesn’t Ask Me To Change.

You’ve Seen My Mood Swings, My Zidd, My Childish Chaos In This Bhari Jawani —
And Yet, Here You Are… Not Once Making Me Feel Like I’m Too Much.
Instead, You Make Me Feel Like I’m Just Enough.
Exactly The Way I Am.

So This Letter?
It’s Not A Thank You.
It’s A Forever.

For The Version Of Me You’ve Helped Bloom,
For The Parts Of Me You Healed Without Even Knowing,
And For Every Moment That Made Us Us...

You’re Not Just A Friend.
You’re My Home In A Human.

With All My Love,
Your Not-So-Silent Bestie 💙
P.S. I Was — And I Still Am — A Hopeless Fan Of Your Smile…
And Your Cutie Little Eyes That Speak Louder Than Words Ever Could. ☁️👀✨
